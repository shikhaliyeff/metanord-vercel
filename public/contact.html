Frequently Asked Questions

What countries do you operate in? ▼

MetaNord OÜ is based in Estonia and serves customers across Europe, with a primary focus on the Baltic states, Nordic countries, and Eastern Europe. We also supply products to select international markets outside Europe. Please contact us for specific information about availability in your country.

Do you provide technical support for your products? ▼

Yes, we provide technical support and documentation for all our products. Our team includes specialists who can assist with product selection, installation advice, and technical specifications. We also offer project consultation services for larger infrastructure projects.

What are your minimum order quantities? ▼

Minimum order quantities vary by product type and specifications. We accommodate both small orders for specific project needs and bulk orders for larger projects or distributors. Please contact our sales team for specific minimum order information regarding the products you're interested in.

How long do deliveries take? ▼

Delivery times depend on product availability, order volume, and destination. Standard deliveries within Europe typically take 3-10 business days, while custom or large orders may require 2-4 weeks. International shipments outside Europe might take longer. We provide estimated delivery times with each quote.

Do you provide product certifications and test reports? ▼

Yes, we provide all necessary product certifications, test reports, and compliance documentation with our products. All our products meet relevant European standards and come with appropriate certification. If you require specific certifications for your project, please inform us when requesting a quote.

MetaNord OÜ is a trading and distribution company specializing in high-quality aluminum profiles and infrastructure products for European and international markets.
