About MetaNord

A leading supplier of high-quality infrastructure products in the European market

MetaNord OÜ is a dynamic trading and distribution company based in Estonia, specialized in aluminum profiles and infrastructure products for various industries. With our extensive network of international suppliers, we offer competitively priced products for construction, utilities, and industrial sectors.

Our commitment to quality and reliability makes us a trusted partner for businesses across Europe and beyond. We provide comprehensive solutions tailored to meet the specific requirements of our clients.
