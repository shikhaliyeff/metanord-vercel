Product Categories

HDPE Pipes

High-density polyethylene pipes for water, sewage, and gas applications with excellent chemical resistance and long service life.

Key Features:

  • Excellent chemical and corrosion resistance
  • Lightweight and flexible for easy installation
  • Temperature resistance from -40°C to +60°C
  • Available in different pressure ratings
  • Nominal size range: DN20 to DN1200

Applications:

  • Potable water supply systems
  • Sewage and drainage systems
  • Gas distribution networks
  • Industrial fluid transport
  • Agricultural irrigation systems

Double Corrugated Pipes

Durable double-wall corrugated pipes designed for storm water drainage and sewage systems with high ring stiffness and load bearing capacity.

Key Features:

  • High ring stiffness classes: SN4, SN8, SN16
  • Excellent resistance to chemicals and abrasion
  • Smooth interior wall for optimal flow
  • Corrugated exterior for increased strength
  • Nominal size range: DN100 to DN1200

Applications:

  • Storm water drainage systems
  • Non-pressure sewage systems
  • Highway drainage
  • Agricultural drainage
  • Low pressure culverts

HSAW Pipes

Helical Submerged Arc Welded pipes manufactured for water transportation, piling applications, and structural purposes with high strength and durability.

Key Features:

  • High-quality steel with various grades available
  • Anti-corrosion coatings (internal and external)
  • High pressure resistance
  • Available with various end preparations
  • Nominal size range: DN300 to DN3000

Applications:

  • Water transportation networks
  • Piling applications for foundations
  • Structural purposes in construction
  • Industrial water systems
  • Marine and offshore constructions

Oil & Gas Pipes

High-pressure steel pipes specifically designed for oil and gas transportation, manufactured according to API 5L and other international standards.

Key Features:

  • High pressure and temperature resistance
  • Meets API 5L, ISO, EN standards
  • Various steel grades available (X42-X70)
  • Special coatings for corrosion protection
  • Nominal size range: DN100 to DN1200

Applications:

  • Oil and gas transmission pipelines
  • Refinery piping systems
  • Natural gas distribution networks
  • Offshore oil and gas extraction
  • Petrochemical processing plants

Manhole Covers

Cast iron manhole covers and frames in various load classes (A15 to F900) for different applications, compliant with EN 124 standard.

Key Features:

  • Load classes: A15, B125, C250, D400, E600, F900
  • Ductile cast iron or gray cast iron construction
  • Anti-slip surface patterns
  • Options with locking mechanisms
  • Clear openings from 300mm to 1000mm

Applications:

  • Pedestrian areas and cycling tracks (A15, B125)
  • Sidewalks and parking areas (C250)
  • Roads and highways (D400)
  • Industrial areas with heavy wheel loads (E600)
  • Airports and port areas (F900)

Road Infrastructure

Comprehensive range of products for road and highway infrastructure including barriers, grates, drainage systems, and traffic management solutions.

Products Include:

  • Cast iron barriers and bollards
  • Rainwater grates and drainage channels
  • Traffic management solutions
  • Road signs and supports
  • Street furniture and urban equipment

Applications:

  • Urban roads and streets
  • Highways and motorways
  • Pedestrian areas and plazas
  • Parking facilities
  • Industrial zones

Valves and Fittings

Wide range of valves, fittings, and accessories for water, sewage, gas, and industrial applications, manufactured according to international standards.

Products Include:

  • Gate valves and butterfly valves
  • Check valves and air release valves
  • Fittings for different pipe materials
  • Couplings and adapters
  • Flange accessories and gaskets

Applications:

  • Municipal water distribution networks
  • Sewage and wastewater systems
  • Industrial processes and cooling systems
  • Fire protection systems
  • Irrigation systems
