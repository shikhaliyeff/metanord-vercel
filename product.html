HDPE pipes (PE pipes)

The Use and Technical Specifications of the Product

High-density polyethylene pipe systems manufactured by "Azertexnolayn" are produced in compliance with the requirements of EN 12201-2 / AZS 806-2 and EN 1555-2 / AZS 805-2 QOST P 50838 standards with available certificates of conformity.
The products are provided with ISO 9001-2015 Quality Management System certification.
PE pipes do not change the odor and taste of water. Therefore, it is provided with health and hygiene certificates.
PE 100 pipes have 50 years of full warranty period with operation at 20°C. In the event that the pipes are used over the above-mentioned temperature, the durability and service-life of the product will be reduced.

Standards

Standards; EN 12201-2 / AZS 806-2 / EN 1555-2 / AZS 805-2 / ГOCT P 50838
Nominal diameter; Ø16-630 mm
Wall thickness; 2-67 mm
Pipe length; 12 000 mm (available in any length)
Row material; PE100
SDR; 6-41
Pressure; pn 4 - pn 32

Advantages

High resistance;
High flow;
Chemical resistance;
Comfortable assembly;
Long-term operation;
